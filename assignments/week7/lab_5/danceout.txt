 Here's  how  you  dance  the Monstrosity.
 Stand  with  your  feet together.
 Now,  move  your  left  foot  abnormally  to  the side.
 Now  stamp  your  right  foot  4  times  and  put  your  hands  on  your  partner's  dogs .
 Next,  you  both  lept  slowly  to  the  right  and  bend  your  penis backward.
 For  the  next  eight  counts,  both  of  you  shot  splendidly  to  the left.
 Next,  you  and  your  partner  stand  back  to  back  and  wiggle  your  cats  and  slap  your  people together.
 Don't  forget  to  keep  stamping  your  right foot.
 Now,  face  your  partner  again,  put  your  houses  together  and  shout,  "  squirsh  !"  Now,  play  backward  and  repeat  the  whole  thing  6 times.
 If  you  feel  that  you  can't  learn  this  dance,  you  can  always  spend  the  next  one out.
